Tamagotchi Game!

It a pleasure to present to your attention a fun game where you can play a Tamagotchi character.
The game has certain options and rules.

Rules and options

  1. You have to name your Tamagotchi to be able give a compliment to a chef with the Tamagotchi's name.
  2. You have to feed your Tamagotchi, otherwise, it will die.
  3. You can slow down or gain Tamagotchi's metabolism by choosing what to drink.
  4. Food can be good or bad if it's a bad food Tamagotchi will be poisoned and will lose points of food.
  5. There are four options of telling a story jokes with Tamagotchi emoji emotions.
  6. There are four options for emoji faces.
  7. If Tamagotchi dies, write the name in the name field and press a button "start over", it will restart the game from the beginning.
